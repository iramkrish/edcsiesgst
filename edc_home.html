Entrepreneurship Development Cell

EDC SIES GST was found in the year 2010 by Mr.Shreeram Suresh under guidance of our faculty incharge Prof.Sumitra Padmanabhan with a motive to equip an individual with all the rudimentary qualities essential for being a successful entrepreneur.
We are home to many students who have dreamt of a start-up plan and have helped them in execution of it from the scrap until it has got well settled in the market. EDC Cell is a proud council with start-ups like Festivus, Cosweb Technologies, PC Assembly Line Co., Orbit Hostings, Naspack in its credits .
We do much more than holdingspeaker sessions, workshops and competitions. We introduce Students to a whole new dimension which they never thought of venturing into.

Bizence , a national level B-plan competition is organized by the EDC for entrepreneur aspirants

Interactive speaker session are conducted round the year,with CEOs,Book Authors etc.

Events are contucted along the length of the year with the motto to introduce engineer with actual marketing environment.
